Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to get approved?

Our approval process is incredibly fast. Most applications are approved within 1 hour during business hours. Once approved, funds can be disbursed to your account within the same day.

What documents do I need to apply?

You'll need a valid South African ID, proof of income (payslips or bank statements), and 3 months of bank statements. All documents can be uploaded directly through our secure application form.

How much can I borrow?

Loan amounts range from R1,000 to R200,000 depending on your income, credit score, and other factors. Use our loan calculator to estimate how much you may qualify for.

What are the repayment terms?

Repayment terms range from 3 to 160 months depending on the loan amount and type. We offer flexible repayment options to suit your financial situation.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All fees and charges are clearly disclosed before you accept the loan. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges.

Frequently Asked Questions

×

How long does the application process take?

Most applications are processed within 24-48 hours. Business loans may take 3-5 business days due to additional documentation requirements.

What documents do I need to apply?

You'll need a valid ID, proof of income (payslips or bank statements), and proof of residence. Additional documents may be required based on the loan type.

Can I apply if I have bad credit?

We consider applications from all credit profiles. While good credit improves your chances, we also look at your current financial situation and ability to repay.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in transparent pricing. All fees and charges are clearly disclosed upfront before you sign any agreement.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes, you can settle your loan early without any penalties. This can help you save on interest charges.

How do I check my application status?

You can track your application status through our online portal or mobile app using your application reference number.
